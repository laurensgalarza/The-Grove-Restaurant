About Us

The Grove was born in 1993, when we left Greece and came to the United States. We found that cooking kept us connected to our home in the Mediterranean, and decided we must share our cuisine with our new community. Two years after arriving in Brooklyn, we opened up The Grove, inspired by the olive groves from our childhood. Ever since then, we have been honored to share our love of food and the Mediterranean with you.
